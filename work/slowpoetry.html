Slow Typography Experiments (WIP)

Words that bloat and sag like a sponge.

I kind of like how the characters begin to glitch and resemble broken landscapes, as if your computer doesn't know what to do with your words. Maybe they're heavy, maybe they index something beyond what symbols can accomplish.

Colors and text are reflected directly in a very long URL. Why have a back-end that serves custom shortened links? That would be like checking your coat into a museum closet forever, or giving someone the lock to your key. There's a simple privacy in hiding amongst the vast universe of all possible combinations of letters.
