Poster for TEDxRutgers, a student-run organization that hosts an annual TEDx conference and other smaller events open to the community.

Because TED is a conference with broad perspectives, we didn't want its visual representation to be confined to specific examples of tech, entertainment, or design. Following the theme of footnotes, we chose instead to depict a process: engaging with something to appreciate it more closely, like we often did as kids. 2017.

Entry to poster/art contest for Japan Day at Central Park.

The brief called for a depiction of "Japanese culture in New York". I took inspiration from tenugui fabric and proposed a drawing made up of patterns that could be remixed across other visual materials. 2017.
