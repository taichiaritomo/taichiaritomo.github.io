Demo!
A simple tool that models visual interest toward textual information. It continuously processes text (left) as an image and generates its saliency map (right). The intensity of white corresponds to the likelihood that a human viewer will gaze at that point first, according to a computer vision algorithm that models visual attention.

I made this with the hypothesis that it could help us better understand the nature of emphasis and the experience of seeing our designed information for the first time.
